Frequently Asked Questions

Is Connect AV secure?

Yes, Connect AV uses the WireGuard protocol, which provides state-of-the-art cryptography. All connections are encrypted end-to-end, and our gateway devices create secure tunnels that only allow authorized access.

Will I need IT approval to install this at client sites?

Usually yes, but we've designed Connect AV to be IT-friendly. Our gateway device connects to your client's network but doesn't modify their security settings. We also provide documentation to help explain the security protocols to IT departments.

How long does it take to set up?

Most installations take less than 30 minutes. The gateway is plug-and-play, and our team provides personal setup assistance for your first client site during the free trial period.

How is this different from TeamViewer or VNC?

Unlike traditional remote desktop solutions that only give you access to a specific computer, Connect AV gives you direct network access to all connected devices at the client site. This means you can use your own software to control displays, DSPs, control systems, and other AV equipment directly.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

After your trial, you can choose from our flexible pricing plans based on the number of client sites you need to access. We'll reach out before your trial ends to help you select the right plan, but there's no obligation to continue.
