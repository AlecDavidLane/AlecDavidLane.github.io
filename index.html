Common Questions

Everything you need to know about transforming your maintenance services.

"Will this replace my technicians?"
No. Our platform makes your technicians more productive and valuable, not replaceable. They'll spend less time on repetitive tasks and more time on high-value work that requires human expertise. Your best techs can support more clients, resulting in better service and higher margins.
"How is this different from what we already do?"
Traditional AV maintenance is reactive and relies on truck rolls. Maintain AV is proactive and enables remote resolution. While you might already offer remote support, our AI-driven platform can predict failures before they happen and automatically fix issues without human intervention—even at 3AM when your team is sleeping.
"How quickly will we see ROI?"
Most partners see positive ROI within the first month. The platform immediately reduces the need for on-site visits (saving travel time and costs) while allowing you to take on more maintenance contracts or charge premium rates for enhanced service. Our partners typically see a 5-10X return on their investment.
"What systems does this work with?"
Our platform works with all major manufacturers including Crestron, Extron, AMX, QSC, Biamp, Shure, and many others. We support both IP-connected and serial-controlled devices through various gateway options. If you have legacy systems, we can work with you to develop appropriate monitoring solutions.
"How do we sell this to our clients?"
We provide complete sales enablement materials including pre-built SLA templates, marketing collateral, and ROI calculators for your clients. You can position this as an "AI-Enhanced Service" that provides faster response times, better uptime, and more detailed system analytics—all things clients are willing to pay premium prices for.
